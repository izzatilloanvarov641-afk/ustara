2. Who can see what

A barber can see the name, phone number, and photos attached to bookings made with them — they need this to actually provide the service. A client's reference or style photo is shown only to the barber they're booking with, never publicly.

Public information includes: a barber's profile (name, photos, services, pricing, location) and reviews left by clients. Ustara does not display a reviewer's name alongside their review.

We do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not share it with advertisers. Ustara currently has no advertising partners; if that changes in the future, this policy will be updated first.

3. Where your data is stored

Ustara's data is stored using Supabase, a third-party database and file storage provider. Photos are stored in Supabase's file storage; everything else is stored in a Supabase database. We don't run our own separate servers for this data.

4. Payments

Ustara does not process card payments or store any payment card information. Clients pay barbers directly. Barbers separately owe Ustara a platform fee per completed booking, tracked inside the app and currently settled manually (bank transfer) — no card numbers are ever entered into or stored by Ustara.

5. Your choices

  • You can update your profile information, saved style preferences, and photos at any time from your account settings.
  • You can remove cancelled, pending, or completed bookings from your own view (this hides them from your list — it doesn't delete the underlying record, since barbers and Ustara's own records may still need it for commission tracking).
  • To request full deletion of your account and personal data, contact us at the email below.

6. Children's privacy

Ustara doesn't knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Bookings for a minor (such as a kids' haircut) must be made through a parent or guardian's own account — the child's own information isn't separately collected as an account holder.

7. Changes to this policy

If how we handle your data changes meaningfully, we'll update this page and note the new date at the top.
