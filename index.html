The problem

The best barbers are booked. The rest are a coin flip.

Every neighbourhood has one great barber — and a waiting bench full of people who found him first. Everyone else cuts hair for strangers who came in because the door was open, not because anyone recommended the chair.

Ustara fixes both sides: clients stop gambling on unknown chairs, and skilled barbers stop depending on foot traffic. Good work becomes visible — and bookable.
